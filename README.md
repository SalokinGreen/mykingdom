# My Kingdom
Welcome to My Kingdom! This project is designed to help you build and manage your own virtual kingdom. You have to rule wisely, make the right dicesions, and manage your needs. One too high or too low and your kingdom will fall into ruin.^

## Features
- Build and expand your kingdom with various structures.
- LLM-powered decision making to guide your kingdom's growth.
- Resource management to ensure your kingdom thrives.
- Interactive gameplay.
